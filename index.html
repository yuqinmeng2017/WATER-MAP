About this project

This is an water supply map of the Jaipur city created in the Scripting Civic Engagement seminar at the University of Virginia (Fall 2017), taught by Andrea Hansen Phillips.

Map data courtesy of Mapbox Studio and the GIS data from the City of Jaipur.
